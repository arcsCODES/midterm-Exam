Home

“Coffee Bliss Cubes” is a product that was developed by a team of five students to come up with a deliciously enjoyable coffee jelly product. The name reflects what makes our product special “bliss” captures the rich and satisfying taste of coffee, while “cubes” highlight the jelly’s smooth and bite sized texture. Unlike other regular coffee drinks, the product introduces the bold taste of coffee into this chewy refreshing experience that a jelly cube presents. Preparing with good-quality ingredients, the serving should meet the best quality of both flavors and textures combined, hence giving Coffee Bliss Cubes something that is definitely worth trying.

We will promote our coffee jelly through word-of-mouth, social media marketing, and direct selling within the school. Engaging posts and appealing visuals will be shared online to attract attention and generate interest. We will also use posters and in-person recommendations to introduce our product to more students and faculty. By maintaining a strong presence and interacting with potential customers, we aim to build excitement and encourage more people to try our coffee jelly. .

Why Choose Coffee Bliss Cube?


1. Product

Our company offers high-quality coffee jelly, crafted to provide a refreshing and unique experience. Made with premium coffee and carefully prepared gelatin, our coffee jelly delivers a smooth, satisfying texture and bold coffee flavor. It’s available in different sizes, making it perfect for any occasion whether as a standalone treat or added to desserts and beverages. The versatile nature of our coffee jelly makes it an ideal choice for coffee enthusiasts looking for something new, delicious, and convenient.

2. Market Size

Our customers include students, teachers, staff, guests, and friends from the Philippine Women’s College (PWC). With a community of over 1,000 potential buyers, we anticipate many will try our coffee jelly either for themselves or as a treat for others. If 30% of them purchase, that’s over 300 sales. Given that our coffee jelly is affordable and offers a unique experience, we expect some customers to make repeat purchases, further increasing our sales.

3. Customers Requirements

Customers want a unique and refreshing coffee experience through our coffee jelly, which must be affordable, high-quality, and convenient. They seek a product that provides a distinct, satisfying texture and flavor, with consistent quality across every serving. Additionally, the product should be easy to store and enjoy, fitting into the busy lifestyles of students and professionals alike. Meeting these needs is essential for building trust and loyalty in our market.
